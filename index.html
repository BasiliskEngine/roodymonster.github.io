Welcome to GoStar! GoStar is a competitive and fast-paced fighting game, focused on high mechanics and micromanagement. GoStar has currently not reached the Alpha stage, but has reached the beginning of it's life time. This homepage will most likely act as the game blog, so that everybody can keep up with it's development. In addition, I am developing plenty of resources to help you guys get started in learning the game, because it is very punishing on in-experienced brawlers. As the developer of GoStar, I am taking focus on creating a meta game and adding new content to ensure that the gameplay and the experience is consistently fluid and flawless for the player. Since I just started this project, allow time before you can grab a quick build of the game, and before any screenshots are leaked. Anyways, I hope everybody gets to be part of and enjoy the competitive fighting experience that is GoStar. - Roody <3
